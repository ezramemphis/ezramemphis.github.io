The Story of a Chopped Little Boy

The Boy Who Thought He Could...

"My enemies tried to silence me, so I switched to interpretive dance"

Once upon a time, there was a boy who truly believed he could do anything — once he dreamed about microwaving a burrito so hot that not even he could eat it. His confidence was unmatched, his haircut questionable, and his optimism slightly delusional.

One day, he decided he would become a great music producer. He downloaded every free plugin known to man and started making beats that sounded like a printer fighting a fax machine. But to him, it was genius.

Then came his next goal: passing Intro to Programming. Unfortunately, the boy thought “debugging” meant swatting flies with a keyboard. Still, he persevered, mostly by just spamming ChatGPT until he was able to get the assignments finished

He decided one day to make a list of all of his dreams, so that he could achieve them one by one. The list included as follows:

  • Getting a girl
  • Becoming a great music producer
  • Affording rent
  • Making his parents proud
  • Having friends
  • Getting good grades
  • Passing Intro to Programming

As time went on, he tried to balance love, music, and rent — all while eating instant noodles like a champion. His parents were a little concerned as he wasn't defeating the bum allegations, but aye, dude was trying.

In the end, the boy didn’t quite achieve everything he wanted. Really not any of them to be frank. But he did achieve something even better: becoming a legend among his friends for his unmatched ability to "almost succeed" at everything he tried. And honestly, that’s inspiring.
